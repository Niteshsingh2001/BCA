Details of the course ↓

Unit No.

CONTENT

1

Basics of Communication: ⇗

  • Definition ☆ Objective ☆ Elements ☆ Roadblocks to effective communication and its remedies ☆ Functions and importance in business.

2

Forms of Communication (Verbal & Non Verbal ) ⇗

  • a) Verbal communication and its types: Written Communication → Oral Communication and their salient features and differences.
  • b) Non verbal communication and its types: Kinesics → Oculesics → Proxemics → Haptics Paralinguistics and chronomics.

3

Grammer: ⇗

  • Noun

3

Effective Writing Skills: ⇗

  • Precise writing ☆ Paragraph writing and dialouge writing.

4

Effective Presentation Skills: ⇗

  • Purpose of making presentation ☆ Principles ☆ Elements ☆ Factors affecting Presentation ☆ Designing and Delivery of presentation.

5

Etiquette : ⇗

  • Social Etiquette and Telephone Etiquette.

6

Personality Development : ⇗

  • Attitude ☆ Factors shaping the attitude ☆ Positive attitude & Negative attitude ☆ SWOT analysis.

Pre-Requisite → Basics of English grammer

Subject Area → Professional Communication

Objective → To enhance the personality and communication skills of the students

Course Outcome

  • A student who successfully fulfills the course requirements will be able to:
    1. Use theories of interpersonal communicationto explain and evaluate their behavior in interpersonal relationships.
    2. Synthesize and apply appropriate and effective conflict management strategies
    3. Understand the reading, writing and listening skills.
    4. Understand the basic structure of inferential resoning passage.
    5. Apply critical reasoning to solve various problems.
