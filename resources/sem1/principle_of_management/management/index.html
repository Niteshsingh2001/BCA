Principle of Management

Management

"Management is defined as process of getting things done by others."

  • The process of reading organization goals by working with and through people and other organizational resources is refers to as a management.
  • Management is an important element in every organization it is the element that coordinates current organization activities and plans for future.

Proper definition

  • Management is the art of knowing what you wan to do and then seeing that is done in the best and cheapest way." -- F W Taylor.
  • Management as a process consist of planning, organising, staffing, directing and controlling the activities perform to determine and accomplish the objectives of organization by the use of people and resource." -- George Terry

Concept of management

Management is the widal concept, so the term used is in different ways.

  1. Management as a discipline.
    • It refers to the field of study having well defined knowleges, concepts and principles which helps in managing the activity.
  2. Management as a group of people.
    • It refers to those people who are managing the organization.
    • For example - XYZ ltd company is good → here we are refering to the management as a group of people of XYZ company those who are managing the organization.
  3. Management as a process
    • It refers to the process or methods of handling activites in an organization.
    • For example - when we say management of ABC is highly decentralized, here we are refering to management as a process.
  4. Integrated approach to management
    • Management is force that integrate resources of the organization into effective operation units by making proper use of management function.
