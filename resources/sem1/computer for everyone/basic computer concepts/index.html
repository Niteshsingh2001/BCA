Basic Computer Concepts

Computer

  • Definition → A computer is a programmable machine designed to perform arithmetic and logical operation automatically and sequentially on the input given by the user and gives the desired output after processing.

Characteristics of computer

  • Accuracy ⇒ Computer always give accurate result depend upon data provided.
  • Speed ⇒ It can perform millions of instructions/operation per second, which takes a lot of time if done manually.
    • Speed of computer is given in nano secconds
      1 nano second = 1 x 10-9sec
  • Reliability ⇒ Computer are more reliable then human beings
    • Computer always produce exact results.
    • The possibility of errors occurs only in the input is wrong i.e. the computers never make mistakes on their own.
  • Versatility → Computers can do different types of work simultaneously. They can perform multiple tasks at the same time.
  • Storage ⇒ As humans can forget things after period of time but computer can store data for long time.

Disadvantages of computer:

  • Lack of common-sense
    • This is one of the major limitations of computer
    • Now matter how efficient, fast and reliable computers might be but yet do not have any common sense because no full-proof algorithm has been designed to programme logic into them.
    • As computers function based on the stored programmes, they simply lack common sense.
  • Zero IQ
    • Computer have zero Intelligence Quotient (IQ).
    • They are unable to see and think the actions to perform in a particular situation unless that situation is already programmed in to them.
    • Computers are programmable to complete each and every task, however small it may be.
  • Lack of decision-making
    • Decision-making is a complicated process involving information, knowledge, intelligence, wisdom, and ability to judje.
    • The computer does not have the ability to make decisions on their own because they do not possess all the essentials of decision-making.

Application of computer

  • Educational Institute
    • It is used to teach children in every school and colleges.
  • Business
    • Used in the field of business so that the business can be made more profitable and the business becomes easier.
  • E-Commerce
    • Companies like Amazon, Flipcart and also food delivery companies are using computers to manage their shipping and transportation.
  • E-learning
    • Many educational resources are uploaded to internet using computers.
  • Research and development
    • Using computer scientists are able to record their research.
  • Online gaming
    • Online games can be played in computer.
  • Banking
    • To save and record transaction of users of that particular banks.

Input/Output

  • Input → Data which goes in CPU for processing.
  • Output → Data which comes out of CPU.

Input Devices

  • Mouse → controls cursor, helps in moving and selecting text, icons, files and folders.
  • Keyboard → inputs characters, number, etc.
  • Barcode reader → reads barcode and collects data related to products.
  • Scanner → scans document and stores in computer
  • Webcam → records video and captures pictures to store inside computer storage.
