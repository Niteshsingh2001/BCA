Pre-Requisite → Knowledge of Computers

Subject Area → Computer Application

Objective → To develop critical thinking skills in students and to make them aware of social media ethics.

Course Outcome

  • Understand critical thinking and identify and develop different sub-skills of critical thinking.
  • Classify different types of social network and identify their recent trends.
  • Evaluate any situation on Social Media critically.
  • Understand social media analytics.
  • Distinguish between ethical/unethical use of social media and understand different social media laws and regulations.
